Our client saved 83% in ticket fees.

The music venue sold presale tickets through a third-party ticketing platform and paid nearly $1,600 in fees on 250 tickets, heavily cutting into event revenue.

"I wound up paying almost $1600. And I'm just flabbergasted. Like, EventBrite is turning into Ticketmaster. That's insane." -Venue Owner

The Backline UX Solution

We redesigned the venue website and embedded native ticket sales directly on their site:

  • On-site checkout experience
  • Lower processing fees by over 80%
  • Same secure purchasing proces for fans

Results

Platform Tickets Ticket Fee Total fees
EventBrite 250 $6.40 $1,600
Venue Site 250 $1.08 $270

Impact

$1,330 saved (83% reduction in fees)

More revenue stayed with the venue - funding future shows instead of platform costs.

Lower fees for patrons to pay - higher conversion rates and return customers.

Takeaways

  • Website-embedded ticketing drastically lowers platform costs
  • Better UX increases presales while protecting margins
  • Venues keep control of their customers and revenue
